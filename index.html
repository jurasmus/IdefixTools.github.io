IdefixHR - A Modern, Identity-Driven HR Platform

IdefixHR is a lightweight, intelligent HR system built for organizations that want full control over their employee lifecycle while reducing manual administration. Designed with an identity‑first mindset, IdefixHR seamlessly connects HR data with Microsoft Entra ID through secure provisioning APIs, ensuring that employee information is always accurate, synchronized, and protected. IdefixHR acts as the central source of truth for your workforce, combining HR master data, salary details, and identity governance into one streamlined platform.
